Common German Slang

Krass
Means "Cool", "Rad", or "Intense"
Quatsch
Means "Nonsense" or "Rubbish"
Na?
Informal greeting meaning "Hey" or "What's up?"
Mach's gut!
Means "Take care!" or "Have a good one!"
Auf jeden Fall
Means "Definitely!" or "For sure!"
Moin
Shortened version of "Guten Morgen" and means "Good morning" (used mainly in northern Germany)
Servus
Means both "Hello" and "Goodbye" (used mainly in southern Germany, specifically in Bavaria)
